Geometry of the Good × Contradiction Debt — Moral Topology

3D moral field with terrain, regime trajectories, and all cases from your CSV. Axes: X=Trust density (capacity factor), Y=Effective repair (R), Z=Rupture probability (=1−R/V).

Concept-to-data mapping

This app maps CD variables into GoG coordinates:
  • X (Trust density): capacity_factor = mean of legitimacy, elite cohesion, institutional capacity.
  • Y (Fulfillment proxy): R = effective repair (repair_avg × capacity_factor). In this CSV, only R is provided; we use it as the best available proxy.
  • Z (Debt proxy): rupture_probability = 1 − R/V, monotonic with contradiction debt.
Interpretation: Lower Z and higher X,Y indicate stable basins; higher Z indicates rupture valleys.

How to add/edit a case

Enter CD inputs; we compute \(R_\text{eff} = \text{repair\_avg}\cdot \text{capacity\_factor}\), \(R/V\), and map to (X,Y,Z).
Defaults: X = capacity_factor, Y = R (effective repair) or repair_avg×capacity_factor for custom, Z = 1 − R/V.
